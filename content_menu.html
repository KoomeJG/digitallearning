Introduction

In a move to solve the problem of low Quality education Lewa Digital Literacy team has decided to provide offline content that is more user friendly to northern Kenya communities that lack access to the internet. This will see more than 10 000 students in the region receive quality eduction as per the Kenya Vision 2030.
